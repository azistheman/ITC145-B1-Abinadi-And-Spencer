What is a TV?

A Television is a machine that transmits visual content, no sound or color, that should be the bare minimum of a television. but of course you won't really find a television that doesn't have color or sound nowadays, but just with those chanegs alone it's showing you how interesting the changes of televisons can be

terms to know:

  • TV screen definitions: Cathode-Ray Tube (CRT), Digital Light Processing (DLP), Plasma Display Panel (PDP), Liquid-Crystal-Display (LCD), Organic Light-Emitting Diode (OLED). Display Resolutions: Low-Definition (LD), Standard-Definition (SD), Enhanced-Definition (ED), High-Definition (HD), Ultra-High-Definition (UHD).

  • Aspect Ratio things: 4:3 and 16:9 ratios, 480p/1080p and 4K, 8K, 16K, Portrait, Widescreen.
